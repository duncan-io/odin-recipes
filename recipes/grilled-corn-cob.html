Grilled Corn on the Cob

Grilled corn on the cob is easy and perfect for every summer barbecue. With foil, butter, salt, and a little pepper, the corn will be perfectly tender and sweet.

Ingredients

  • 6 corn ears
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Step 2: Peel back corn husks and remove silk. Place 1 tablespoon butter, salt and pepper on each piece of corn. Close husks.
  3. Step 3: Wrap each ear of corn tightly in aluminum foil.
  4. Step 4: Cook on the preheated grill, turning occasionally, until tender, about 30 minutes.
